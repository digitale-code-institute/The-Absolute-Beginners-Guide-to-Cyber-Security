Welcome to the section review. Below are the key takeaways from this section

  • It's recommended to backup your computer occasionally and it's always better to have more than one backup
  • Storing data in the cloud can provide convenience and boost productivity. However it could provide major issues such as trust, access to data as well as data ownership.
  • Encryption transforms regular text into cipher text and such text can only be read through the use of adecryption key.
  • Encryption will not provide protection against malware attacks
  • The Direct Memory Access attack is the most common method used by hackers to defeat encryption
